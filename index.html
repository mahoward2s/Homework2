About Me

Hello everyone, my name is Matthew Howard. I am a St. Louis native working towards becoming a Full Stack Web Developer. I have multiple hobbies that I enjoy. A few of them are hiking, rock climbing, playing bass guitar, painting/drawing, and just overall creating things.

I have travelled to Spain in whcih I fell absolutely in love with. I have travelled to almost every state in the USA. I have seen the St. Louis Blues play in 7 arenas.
